About us

Nordic Electric is established in Stockholm - the capital of Sweden in the heart of Europe. It was created to efficiently manage, export and distribute the European standard electrical bulk material and equipment on a global basis. It is a one-stop-store and prime source accommodating all your business requirement of Electrical products.

Nordic Electric coordinates professionally the sourcing, procuring, marketing, sales, supplying, shipping, and collection shipment on an in-house basis for globally leading manufacturers in the industrial, commercial & residential market for the above said line products. We export, import and stock a superior quality material with international recognised standards.

Nordic Electric exports and supplies products globally to distributors, wholesalers, contractors, developers, EPC companies, end users, government and non-government organisations.
